Salman F Rahman

Businessman & industrialist

Biography

Salman Fazlur Rahman is a renowned industrialist and social worker, better known as Salman F Rahman.He is the co-founder and vice chairman of Beximco Group.He was elected as a Member of Parliament from Dhaka-1 constituency in the 2016 Jatiya Sangsad elections and is playing an important role as a full minister as a private industry and investment adviser to Hon'ble Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina. His dynamic leadership and far-reaching initiatives have reshaped the country's business environment and made a significant contribution to achieving economic growth.Salman F Rahman, son of father Fazlur Rahman and mother Syeda Fatina Rahman.His wife Syeda Rubaba Rahman and only son Ahmed Shayan Fazlur Rahman.

POLITICS

Salman F Rahman's political career began in the mid-nineties. At that time he formed a political party called Prosperous Bangladesh Movement. He later joined the Bangladesh Awami League. In the 2016 Jatiya Sangsad elections, Salman F Rahman contested from Dhaka-1 constituency comprising Dohar and Nawabganj on behalf of Bangladesh Awami League and was elected as a Member of Parliament after winning by a huge margin.He is currently holding the important post of Full Minister as Private Industry and Investment Adviser to Hon'ble Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina.
